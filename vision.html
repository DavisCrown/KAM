OUR VISION

Vision Statement

At Kingdom Ambassadors Mission, our vision is to see a world transformed by Kingdom values, where individuals and communities reflect the love of Christ.

Mission Statement

Our mission at Kingdom Ambassadors Mission is to represent and facilitate the manifestation of God’s eternal Kingdom on earth. We achieve this by training and equipping churches and leaders for transformational leadership and impactful mission work, seeking to spread the Gospel to all nations.

Motto

"Proclaiming Truth, Igniting Souls, Transforming Communities"

This motto captures our commitment to spreading the Gospel message, igniting spiritual fervor in individuals' hearts, and transforming communities through the love of Christ. Through evangelism, discipleship, and active community engagement, we empower individuals to embrace Kingdom values, ultimately contributing to the establishment of vibrant Kingdom Communities worldwide.
