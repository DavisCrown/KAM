Meet the Team

Meet the team behind Kingdom Ambassadors
Missions—passionate leaders committed to
guiding others in their journey to know God,
discover purpose, and live on mission.

The team is led by Patrick Nsanzamahoro who serves
the mission as our Founder & Managing Director.

Patrick Nsanzamahoro

Founder & Executive Director

Nsanzamahoro Patrick is the founder of Kingdom Ambassadors Mission and a devoted servant of God, dedicated to reaching all nations with the gospel, self-discovery, and leadership development. With extensive experience as a missionary for Youth with a Mission (YWAM), he has transformed lives across East Africa, particularly in Rwanda, Kenya, Uganda, and Tanzania. Patrick holds a master’s degree in international business and economic law and a diploma in theology. He is a respected lawyer and lecturer, as well as a consultant, using his expertise to advocate for justice and empowerment in his communities. Passionate about making a difference, Patrick inspires others through his commitment to faith and service.

MURENZI Janvier

Director of Networks

Murenzi Janvier has been a full-time minister and pastor for over 30 years, serving in various churches throughout the Eastern Province of Rwanda. Dedicated to evangelism and expanding the Kingdom, Murenzi leads external networking activities at Kingdom Ambassadors Mission, engaging with senior church leaders, strategic Christian leaders, charities, parliamentarians, and beyond to share the heart and vision of KAM.

Nzogiroshya Theoneste

Director of Ministries

Nzogiroshya Theoneste is an administrator by profession and a teacher by calling, dedicated to discipleship and leadership development. He previously served with IFES-Rwanda as Student and Graduate Ministry Coordinator and was an elder at Gospel Community Church for nine years. Currently, he is part of the Simply Mobilizing Rwanda National Coordination team and serves as Director of Operations at Profit Path Advisors Ltd. Theoneste joins Kingdom Ambassadors Mission as Director of Ministries.

NTIRENGANYA Ezekias

Training & Mentoring Director

Ntirenganya Ezekias has served with Scripture Union Rwanda for over 10 years, leading various programs and heading the training team. He joined Kingdom Ambassadors Mission as a Training & Mentoring Director, helping to establish the KAM Pioneer Programme. In addition to his extensive experience in training and mentoring, he has also worked with World Vision. Holding a master's degree in theology, Ntirenganya is a dedicated teacher by profession. As the Training & Mentoring Director at Kingdom Ambassadors Mission, he is committed to strengthening impact and expanding the reach of quality training and mentoring initiatives within the organization.

UWAMAHORO Claudine

Director of Property & Finance

Uwamahoro Claudine joined Kingdom Ambassadors Mission in 2024 with a passion for maximizing the resources entrusted to KAM to further God's kingdom in Rwanda and beyond. Prior to her work with KAM, Claudine brought over 5 years of experience working with various charities and not-for-profit organizations, where she played a pivotal role in transforming their operations for greater efficiency and impact.

ABAHAWENASE Diane

Administration Officer

Diane joined Kingdom Ambassadors Mission as the Head of Communications, driven by a deep desire to see every person reached with the life-transforming message of the Gospel. With a strong focus on evangelism and discipleship, Diane seeks to engage and inspire others through compelling storytelling and outreach initiatives. With a passion for mentoring and prayer, she is dedicated to being a vessel for God's glory and advancing His Kingdom wherever He leads.

TUYISHIMIRE Deogratias

Head of IT

Deogratias joined Kingdom Ambassadors Mission in 2024 with a heart to drive the organization’s IT initiatives and make the most of the technological resources KAM is endowed with, to grow God’s kingdom in Rwanda and beyond. Prior to his work with KAM, Deogratias gained over 5 years of experience working with various charities and not-for-profit organizations, helping them to transform their operations through effective technology solutions.
