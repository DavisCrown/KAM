TRAINING, EQUIPPING CHURCHES FOR MISSION AND TRANSFORMATION LEADERSHIP

We are committed to empowering communities to discover their purpose in life, embrace their unique callings and gifts, and unlock their potential for evangelism especially among those who are least likely to hear the gospel in their local neighborhoods.

Our trained and experienced missionaries are enthusiastic about helping individuals and groups build confidence and effectiveness in sharing the transformative message of Jesus.

Our Training Programs

We offer a diverse selection of training courses tailored specifically for church groups and various organizations seeking to enhance their knowledge on a range of topics, including leadership and more.

Each program is designed to empower participants and equip them with the skills they need to thrive.

Practical Evangelism Training (PET) days are held throughout the year, focusing on an area of urban and rural evangelism. We host conferences, giving your church a chance to go into these topics in more depth to make connections with like-minded Christians.

If your church wants to increase their confidence in talking to Muslim neighbours, our Muslim Engagement Training is a great way of doing this.

Find out about these and all our other training courses.

Bespoke Church Trainings

You may also have a specific training need as a church. Perhaps there is a community close to your church that God has put on your heart to reach out to.

We are always open to discussing bespoke training sessions. Depending on location, this might be a one-off event or a series of sessions, customized specially for your church and your context.

Our team of experienced evangelists have a breadth of urban and rural mission experience and can adapt to apply this to help your church. They can provide practical advice, teaching on biblical foundations and even going out into the community with you, practically demonstrating how to effectively engage in gospel conversations.

Contact us using the form below and we'll be in touch to chat through your ideas for local mission and any support you might need.

EXAMPLES OF OUR PROGRAMS

Pre-Trip Training

We offer comprehensive training sessions for ministers and teams preparing to embark on evangelistic missions across Africa. These sessions cover essential topics such as cross-cultural communication, effective evangelism strategies, and understanding the local context. Participants are equipped with the tools they need to engage with communities meaningfully and respectfully.

Teams from Around the World

We welcome teams from various countries who seek to participate in evangelical outreach. These teams often include medical professionals providing health care, educators conducting literacy programs, and musicians leading worship. Their diverse skills and backgrounds enrich our missions and help meet the varied needs of the communities we serve.​​

Through Go Africa Evangelism

We enable a holistic approach to outreach that combines Gospel proclamation with tangible acts of service, fostering transformation in individuals and communities across Africa. By connecting Christian ministers and teams with these opportunities, we are working together to spread the Good News and cultivate a new generation of believers committed to making a difference.
