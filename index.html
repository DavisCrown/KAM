We partner with the local church to share the gospel and equip everyday believers to boldly and lovingly bring the hope of Christ to those who need it most.

KINGDOM AMBASSADORS MISSIONS IS A FAITH-DRIVEN MOVEMENT EQUIPPING BELIEVERS TO DISCOVER THEIR IDENTITY AND PURPOSE IN CHRIST.
