Volunteer

Are you looking for ways to serve God and broaden your horizons?

Are you passionate about sharing the love of Christ and helping others discover their purpose? Kingdom Ambassadors Missions is looking for dedicated volunteers to serve alongside us in various ways.

Whether it's street evangelism, organizing events, media and communications, mentorship, discipleship, or community outreach, there's a place for you to make an impact.

As a volunteer, you'll grow in your faith, develop your gifts, and be part of a mission that transforms lives. Join us today and be the hands and feet of Christ in your community and beyond.
