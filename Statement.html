STATEMENT OF FAITH

As evangelical Christians, we
uphold the following core doctrines as essential to the Christian faith—truths that lead us to live with love, serve others practically, and share the gospel with conviction.

At Kingdom Ambassadors Mission (KAM), we affirm our unwavering belief in the foundational truths of the Christian faith revealed in Scripture. We strive to embody the teachings and principles of our faith in every aspect of our lives and ministry.

I. The Nature of God

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit (Matthew 28:19; John 10:30; Ephesians 4:4-6). This triune God is the creator and sustainer of all life and the ultimate source of truth and love.

II. The Lordship of Jesus Christ

We affirm the deity of our Lord Jesus Christ, acknowledging His virgin birth (Matthew 1:23), sinless life, miraculous works, atoning death (Romans 5:8), bodily resurrection (1 Corinthians 15:3-4), ascension to the right hand of the Father (Acts 1:11), and His imminent personal return in power and glory. We hold that salvation is found only in Him (John 14:6; Acts 4:12).

III. The Authority of Scripture

We recognize the Bible as the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God, guiding us in faith and practice, and revealing His love for humanity (2 Timothy 3:16-17; 1 Thessalonians 2:13).

IV. The Human Condition and Salvation

We believe that all humanity is fallen and in need of salvation (Romans 3:23). Repentance of sin and faith in Jesus Christ lead to regeneration by the Holy Spirit, resulting in eternal life for the believer (Ephesians 2:8-9). Additionally, we affirm that God will reward the righteous with eternal life in Heaven and that the unrighteous will face everlasting punishment (Matthew 25:46; Luke 24:46-47).

V. The Ministry of the Holy Spirit

We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit, whose indwelling empowers Christians to lead godly lives (Acts 1:8).

VI. The Resurrection and Eternal Life

We affirm the resurrection of both the saved and the lost; the saved to eternal life and the lost to eternal condemnation (1 Corinthians 15:51-57; Revelation 20:11-15).

VII. The Unity of Believers

We believe in the spiritual unity of all believers in Christ. As members of His body, the Church, we are called to support one another in faith (1 Corinthians 12:12, 27; Ephesians 4:2-3).

VIII. The Great Commission

We see evangelism and discipleship as essential responsibilities of all followers of Jesus Christ, tasked with sharing the message of salvation and helping others grow in their faith (Matthew 28:18-20; Acts 1:8).

IX. God's Design for Marriage and Family

We believe that God's plan for human sexuality is to be expressed exclusively within the context of marriage—a sacred union between one genetic male and one genetic female (Genesis 2:24; Matthew 19:5-6). We uphold marriage as the foundational structure of society and the family.

X. The Value of Human Life

We affirm the sacredness of human life from conception to natural death, committing ourselves to care for the physical and spiritual needs of all people (Psalm 139:13; Jeremiah 1:5).

XI. God's Design for Gender

We believe that God inherently and immutably creates each person as either biological male or female. These two distinct but complementary sexes reflect the image of God, and rejecting one's biological sex constitutes a denial of God's creative order (Genesis 1:26-27).

XII. Our Commitment to Prayer and Service

We are dedicated to a life of prayer, seeking God's guidance and strength as we serve Him and our communities (Matthew 9:35–38; Acts 1:8). We embrace our calling to share the Gospel and minister to those in need, reflecting Christ’s love in action.


In all these truths, we seek to glorify God and advance His Kingdom on Earth as faithful ambassadors of Jesus Christ.
