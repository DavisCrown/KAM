WONDERMAS CHILD PROGRAM

A Gift of Love and Hope on Christmas

But Jesus said, 'Let the little children come to me and do not hinder them, for to such belongs the kingdom of heaven.'" 

- Matthew 19:14 (ESV)

The Wondermas Child Program, a vital initiative of Kingdom Ambassadors Mission (KAM), offers a hands-on opportunity to bless children in need around the world. By filling shoeboxes with toys, personal care items, school supplies, and fun gifts, we strive to demonstrate God's love in tangible ways.

EXAMPLES OF OUR WORK

Creating Joyful Shoeboxes

Volunteers gather to fill shoeboxes with carefully selected items that will bring joy to children. Each box includes toys, hygiene products, school supplies, and personal gifts, all representing acts of kindness and love inspired by Jesus.

Distribution Events

We organize distribution events in collaboration with local churches, where children receive their shoeboxes in a celebratory manner. These events not only provide gifts but also create an atmosphere of hope and love, allowing volunteers and recipients to connect on a personal level.

Sharing the Gospel

During the distribution of shoeboxes, local pastors and church leaders take the opportunity to share the Gospel message with children and their families. This creates a powerful moment where the love of Christ is communicated alongside the physical gifts.

Empowering Local Churches

The project empowers local churches to build relationships within their communities. As they distribute shoeboxes, they establish trust and open doors for continued ministry, encouraging children and families to participate in church activities and grow in their faith.

Our Goal

The overarching goal of the Wondermas Child Project is to not only provide material assistance but also to foster spiritual growth among children in the world.

We aim to lead them to become faithful followers of Christ, who in turn will share their faith and experiences with others, creating a ripple effect of love and discipleship.

By participating in the Wondermas Child Project, you are engaging in a meaningful, hands-on experience that blesses children across the globe, showcasing God's unwavering love and compassion.

Together, we can help children experience the joy of giving and receiving, rooted in the love of Christ.
